BENCHMARK INTELLIGENCE

Bengal vs. Global Success Models

Evidence from world-class development hubs that inform this blueprint

🇸🇬

Singapore Jurong Model

Integrated industrial township with radical governance transparency transformed marshland into Asia's industrial core within 20 years.

Bengal Parallel: Delta terrain + strategic port = identical potential, unlocked by institutional trust.

🇮🇳

Gujarat GIFT City

Single-window clearance + transparent regulatory environment attracted over 1 lakh crore in financial sector investment within 8 years.

Bengal Parallel: Same model + blockchain layer eliminates the trust gap entirely.

🇧🇩

Bangladesh Export Zones

Focused agro-industrial export zones grew textile exports from near zero to $47 billion — leveraging the same Bangladesh border Bengal shares.

Bengal Parallel: Cross-border logistics corridor is Bengal's unfair geographic advantage.

CLIMATE RESILIENCE FRAMEWORK

Green Growth, Not Grey Growth

Bengal's Sundarbans, Gangetic delta, and cyclone exposure demand a development model that is climate-proof by design — not as an afterthought.

Mandatory Green Hydrogen & Solar Integration

All industrial zones powered 40% by renewable energy by Year 3. Green Hydrogen export corridor to Southeast Asia.

Mangrove & Wetland Protection Covenant

Zero net deforestation pledge embedded in all land acquisition MoUs, monitored by public satellite dashboard.

Climate Resilience Impact Score (CRIS)

Every project rated on carbon intensity, water stress, and biodiversity impact before approval — publicly visible on the transparency dashboard.

LEGISLATIVE FOUNDATION

The Legal Architecture

Transformative development requires transformative law. Six proposed legislative anchors.

⚖️

Bengal SEZ Amendment Act

Time-bound labour flexibility, sector-specific tax incentives, and 3-year policy stability covenant for investors.

🔗

Public Blockchain Accountability Act (PBAA)

All government contracts above 1 crore on public distributed ledger with real-time citizen access.

🌾

Bengal Agri-Value Chain Development Act

Legal framework for FPO formation, cold chain incentives, and smallholder farmer contract protection.

🎓

Skill Certification & Industry Placement Act

Compels industry partners to absorb minimum 70% of certified graduates with salary guarantees.

📦

Multimodal Logistics Authority of Bengal (MLAB)

Statutory body coordinating port, rail, road, and digital infrastructure under single command.

🌏

Act-East Corridor Trade Framework

Bilateral trade facilitation with Bangladesh and ASEAN gateway states — Bengal as India's eastern export hub.
